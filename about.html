I started playing at nine years old on a secondhand upright that was always slightly out of tune. Trying to fix it myslef is how I ended up learning the other half of this trade.

A little history

Bartolomeo Cristofori built the first piano in Florence around 1700. He called it a gravicembalo col piano e forte &mdash: a harpsichord with soft and loud — because unlike the harpischord, it could play quietly or loudly depnding on how hard you struck the key. That single idea is why we still play it three hundred years later.
